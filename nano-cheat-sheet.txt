ctrl+x : exit nano
ctrl+w : find a string in the text file
ctrl+k : cuts the line being worked on
ctrl+a : moves to the beginning of the line being worked on
ctrl+e : moves to the end of the line being worked on
esc+g : jumps to a specific spot in the text file
esc+$ : enables or disables soft-wrapping of overlong lines (text on the same line will appear on the next line if line becomes too large for terminal window)
esc+l : enables or disabled soft-wrapping of overlong lines (text on the same line will appear to be on the next line, does not cut off words)

Options:
-$ : enables soft-wrapping
-i : automatically indents new lines to the precious line's indentation
